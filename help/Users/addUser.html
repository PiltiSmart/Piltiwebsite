PILTISMART ADD USER

STEP 1

To view the Users, Click on the Dashboard

STEP 2

Select the Users option available.

STEP 3

Click on the add user option available at the bottom of the page.

STEP 4

A pop-up will appear asking essential credenetials.

STEP 5

After filling the essential credentials, proceed to click the Add User Button.

STEP 6

A conformation message tab will appear, press OK and continue.

STEP 7

You can notice the new user is listed in the user section, after refreshing the page.
